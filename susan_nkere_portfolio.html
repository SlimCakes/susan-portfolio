My Design Process
01
Discover & Research
I start by understanding the user — their goals, pain points, and context. Through interviews, flows, and competitive audits, I build a clear foundation before touching Figma.
02
Define & Structure
Information architecture, user flows, and wireframes. I map out the experience systematically so every screen has a clear purpose and logical path.
03
Design & Prototype
High-fidelity UI with consistent design systems. I build interactive prototypes that feel real enough to test — and look polished enough to inspire confidence.
04
Test & Iterate
I test with real users, gather feedback, and refine. Design isn't finished at handoff — it gets better with every round of honest critique.
